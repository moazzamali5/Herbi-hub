Care of this plant

Full Sun

  • Required at least six hour of sunlight
  • Afternoon sun more stronger than morning sun, the plant is better to absorb the afternoon sun tends.
  • Place the plant at sunny areas

Moderate Watering

  • During the dry season, you can water a plant moderately
  • Water the plants once time a week

Fertilizer

  • Seedling is prefer using 30g fartilizer with good watered environment
  • Applying appropriate fertilizer around the tree drip line
  • To avoid root burn, do not apply fertilizer closer with the trunk
